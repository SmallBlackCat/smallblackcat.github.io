Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

The App generates atomic time signals on your iPhone to sync radio-controlled watches. The app is designed to work completely offline — no account, no tracking, no data collection.

One-sentence summary: The App does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What We Don't Collect

The App does none of the following:

  • No account or registration
  • No location data (GPS or coarse)
  • No device identifiers (IDFA, advertising ID, etc.)
  • No analytics or crash reporting
  • No advertisements or ad SDKs
  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)

Local Storage

The App stores a small amount of data on your device only, using iOS system storage. This never leaves your phone:

  • Premium purchase status — remembers you've bought the lifetime upgrade
  • Preferences & history — your selected region, signal duration, and past sync results

Microphone

The app may ask for microphone access only when you tap "Environment Check." It measures ambient noise to assess if your environment is suitable for sync. The audio is processed in memory and discarded immediately — it is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

You can deny microphone permission and still use all core features.

In-App Purchases

Payments are handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We never see or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all transaction data.

Third-Party Services

The App contains no third-party SDKs — no analytics, no ads, no crash reporters, no social media SDKs. The app is fully self-contained.

Data Deletion

To remove all local data, delete the app from your iPhone. That's it — there is no server-side data to erase.

Changes

If this policy is updated, the "Last Updated" date will change. We'll notify you through the app if any material changes are made.
